Ground Rules

be safe All information in this app are community-provided and not validated by any experts, medical or otherwise. Take caution and refer to other sources before doing anything that might harm you.
keep things honest & good This app will only work if people provide useful, correct information. It’s up to you to keep things honest and correct.
Don’t be offensive.
don’t sell or buy anything here This is not a marketplace. There are plenty of other places on the internet to buy and sell things.
nothing personal Don’t reveal any identifying information about yourself on this service.
Don’t post photos of yourself or other people.
If you see a post with someone else’s personally identifying information, flag it.
